## PhD 论文 / Doctoral Thesis

**Computational Simulation of Radiation Damage in Grain Boundaries and Phase Boundaries in Metal Materials**

- 🎓 **机构** / Institution: 山东大学 (Shandong University)
- 📅 **时间** / Period: 2020 – 2025
- 👤 **导师** / Supervisor: Prof. Ning Gao (高宁)
- 🎯 **方向** / Field: Condensed Matter Physics (凝聚态物理)

## 摘要 / Abstract

This thesis applies atomistic simulation methods (Molecular Dynamics and Density Functional Theory) to systematically study the radiation damage mechanisms at grain boundaries (GBs) and phase boundaries (PBs) in metal materials. Topics include defect formation, defect-interface interactions, dislocation network evolution, and the role of interfaces in mitigating radiation-induced degradation in structural materials for nuclear applications.

## 主要贡献 / Key Contributions

- 揭示晶界/相界面对辐照缺陷的吸收与湮灭机制
- 提出周期性位错网络增强 W/Fe 界面抗辐照性能（发表于 Acta Materialia 2025）
- 探索马氏体相变在辐照条件下的适应性响应（发表于 Nature Communications 2025）
